# Quality Criteria — Asufarma Edu Content Scoring

## Overview

Each piece of content is scored across 6 dimensions on a scale of 1-10.
Minimum acceptable score: **7/10 per criterion**.
Overall minimum average: **7.5/10**.
Content below threshold must be revised before publishing.

---

## Criterion 1 — Content Accuracy (Precisão do Conteúdo)

Evaluates whether health claims, ingredient information, and scientific context are correct.

| Score | Description |
|---|---|
| 9-10 | All claims cited or citable; no oversimplification that distorts meaning |
| 7-8 | Claims are accurate; minor simplifications acceptable for audience |
| 5-6 | One claim is imprecise but not harmful; needs correction |
| 3-4 | Multiple inaccuracies or one significant misleading statement |
| 1-2 | Contains false health information or unsupported therapeutic claims |

**Red flags (auto-fail):** "cura", "trata [doença]", dosagens sem embasamento, confundir suplemento com medicamento

---

## Criterion 2 — Brand Voice Alignment (Voz de Marca)

Evaluates whether the content sounds like Asufarma: accessible, warm, human, educational without being cold.

| Score | Description |
|---|---|
| 9-10 | Feels unmistakably Asufarma; emoji use natural; tone matches chosen persona |
| 7-8 | Mostly on-brand; minor inconsistencies in emoji or register |
| 5-6 | Neutral — could be any pharmacy; lacks personality |
| 3-4 | Off-brand — either too clinical/corporate or too casual/slang-heavy |
| 1-2 | Brand voice absent or clearly misaligned |

**Core markers:** coracao azul emoji (💙), coloquial-mas-correto, Salvador identity when appropriate, empathy over authority

---

## Criterion 3 — Engagement Potential (Potencial de Engajamento)

Evaluates how likely this content is to generate saves, shares, comments, and profile visits.

| Score | Description |
|---|---|
| 9-10 | Strong hook in slide 1; clear save-worthy value; explicit CTA with low friction |
| 7-8 | Good hook; useful content; CTA present but generic |
| 5-6 | Hook is weak or delayed; CTA missing or buried |
| 3-4 | No hook; content starts with brand intro or product name |
| 1-2 | Content provides no reason to interact or return |

**Save-worthiness test:** Would someone screenshot this to use later? If no, rethink the value proposition.
**CTA hierarchy:** Salva > Compartilha > Comenta > Segue

---

## Criterion 4 — ANVISA Compliance (Conformidade Regulatória)

Evaluates adherence to Brazilian pharmaceutical advertising regulations.

| Score | Description |
|---|---|
| 9-10 | Fully compliant; disclaimer present and readable; no therapeutic promises |
| 7-8 | Compliant; disclaimer slightly small or less prominent but present |
| 5-6 | Disclaimer missing but content borderline; no explicit violation |
| 3-4 | Missing required disclaimer; or borderline therapeutic promise |
| 1-2 | Clear ANVISA violation: therapeutic claim, disease cure/treatment statement |

**Non-negotiable:** Any content involving internal use of active compounds must include the disclaimer.
Disclaimer minimum size: legible on mobile (≥ 10pt equivalent, not hidden in visual clutter).

---

## Criterion 5 — Visual Design Quality (Qualidade Visual)

Evaluates slide text hierarchy, readability, brand color usage, and overall visual clarity.

| Score | Description |
|---|---|
| 9-10 | Clean hierarchy; one key message per slide; brand colors used correctly; readable on mobile |
| 7-8 | Mostly clean; minor text overload on one slide; colors mostly correct |
| 5-6 | 2-3 slides with too much text; visual hierarchy unclear |
| 3-4 | Most slides are text-heavy; hard to read on mobile; no clear hierarchy |
| 1-2 | Wall of text; brand colors absent; unreadable on mobile |

**Brand colors:** Verde oliva #96b472, Azul marinho #073263, Bege claro #f5f2e8, Turquesa #6dbfb8
**Rule of thumb:** If you squint at the slide and can't identify the main message in 2 seconds, it fails.

---

## Criterion 6 — Platform Optimization (Otimização para Instagram)

Evaluates caption structure, hashtag strategy, and Instagram-native behavior.

| Score | Description |
|---|---|
| 9-10 | First 125 chars are hook+value; hashtags 5-15, niche+broad mix; no banned tags |
| 7-8 | Caption good; hashtags slightly generic or slightly over/under count |
| 5-6 | Caption starts with brand name or product; hashtags generic only |
| 3-4 | Caption reads like a press release; no hashtag strategy |
| 1-2 | Caption is absent, broken, or filled with only hashtags |

**First 125 chars rule:** Instagram truncates captions here. The hook must fit before "ver mais".
**Hashtag strategy:** 3-5 niche (#manipulados #farmaciadermanipulacao #melatoninabrasil), 3-5 mid (#saude #bemestar), 2-3 broad (#salvador #farmacia). Max 15 total.

---

## Overall Scoring Summary

| Average | Decision |
|---|---|
| 8.5-10 | Publish as-is |
| 7.5-8.4 | Publish with minor tweaks noted |
| 6.5-7.4 | Revise one specific criterion and re-score |
| Below 6.5 | Full revision required before publishing |
